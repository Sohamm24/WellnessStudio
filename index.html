-CHANGE YOUR PHYSIQUE IN 42 DAYS: Starting may be a bit difficult, but you will come up with time. The plan will majorly focus 30% on workouts and 70% on diet and lifestyle. Start your goal with easy tasks and later increase your efforts week by week. You can also rate yourself based on how you followed each essential step.

WEEK-1
Sleep well: at least sleep for 7-8hrs a day Don`t eat less, eat right: reduce carbs to 50% of your daily consumption.
Avoid Sugar (Golden rule) Below are some ways to replace carbs in Indian diet.
1.barley, sorghum, barley, Oats flour instead of wheat floor
2.Brown rice instead of white
Maintain your daily step count to 6000 steps

WEEK-2
1.Increase daily step count to 7000 steps
2.Drink 9-10 glasses of water each day

3.Avoid oil completely
4.Leafy vegetables instead of potatoes

WEEK-3
1.Increase daily step count to 8000 steps
2.Make 3 windows of 1 hour.Eat all the food in the specified time of 1 window,do not eat in between.
3.Start doing planks depending on your body size. It may vary from 30s-45s.

WEEK-4
Follow the same tasks which you were following upto week 3.
Increase the plank timings to 8500

WEEK-5
1. Increase daily step count to 9000 steps
2.Indulge yourself in Swimming, Skipping, Battle Ropes, Jogging,
3.Add Healthy fats and Proteins in your diet to gain some muscles and lift some weights daily

WEEK-6
This is the final week,Congratulations you have made it up to the final week and
you may already notice many changes in your body.
Follow the same diet and workout.
Also, you may increase the step count to 10000 steps.

Some additional tips

  1. Set realistic goals
    • Loosing 1-2 pounds a week
    • Avoiding our favourite food items completely is difficult so start avoding slowly
      as you get familiar,you may follow a very strict diet
  2. Get out of your comfort zone
